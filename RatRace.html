How to Play
The Goal

Each player picks a win condition. Be the first to reach your goals! Win as "The Millionaire" by saving $5,000, or "The Scholar" by maxing your Education. You decide!

Action Points

Every week you get 40 Action Points (AP). Moving to a building costs 5 AP. Activities cost 5–25 AP. When you're out of AP, end your turn. Unused AP is lost!

Stats

Track 4 stats: 💵 Money (pay rent & buy things), 🎓 Education (needed for better jobs), 💼 Job Level (how high you climb), 😊 Happiness (don't let it hit zero!)

Locations

Visit GloboCorp to work, School to study, Employment Agency to find jobs, Entertainment for happiness, and Home to rest. Manage your money for rent each week!

Jobs

Start as Janitor and work up to Executive! You need Education and the right clothes for each job tier. Work shifts to earn XP and get promoted automatically.

Jones (AI Rival)

Jones is your rival AI opponent. He's always competing! On Hard mode he's very smart. Watch his progress and try to beat him to your goal!
